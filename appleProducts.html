iPad

iPad is a line of tablet computers designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc., which run the iOS mobile operating system. The first iPad was released on April 3, 2010, the most recent iPad models are the iPad (2018), released on March 27, 2018, the 10.5-inch (270mm) and 12.9 inch (330mm) 2G iPad Pro releaased on June 13, 2017. The user interface is built around the device's multi-touch screen, including a virtual keyboard. All iPads can connect via Wi-Fi; some models also have cellular connectivity.

iPhoneX

iPhone X is a smartphone designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc. It was announced on September 12, 2017, alongside the iPhone 8 Plus at the Steve Jobs Theater in the Apple Park campus. The phone was released on November 3, 2017. This device marks the iPhone series' tenth anniversary, with "X" being the symbol for "ten" in Roman Numerals.

MacBook Air

The MacBook is a line of Macintosh subnotebook computers developed and manifactured by Apple Inc. It consists of a full-size keyboard, a machined aluminium case, and a thin light structure. The Air is available with a screen size of (measured diagonally) 13.3in (33.782 cm), with different specifications produced by Apple. Since 2011, all MacBook Air models have used solid-state drive storage and Intel Core i5 or i7 CPUs.
